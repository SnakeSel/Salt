HOW TO HOTKEY ITEMS
To bind an item to a keyboard hotkey, hover your mouse over the item and press a number (0-9).

To bind an item to a controller hotkey, navigate to the item you wish to bind and hold down one (or both) of the bumper buttons and press any of the directions on the directional pad or any of the four primary buttons. Here are three examples of valid hotkey bindings for an item:

Left Bumper + A
Right Bumper + DPad Up
Left Bumper + Right Bumper + X.
