Crafting an ocarina is no task for a simpleton. This should only be done by those who have achieved mastery in the art of song and craftmanship. 

Components Needed: 

- Blue Whale Oil
- Vibrant Moon Ash
- Fine Oakwood

Instructions: 

Mix one to one ratios. Once crafted, the oakwood needs to sit in the smoke of a slowly dying for a few minutes. Let cool under the night sky.
